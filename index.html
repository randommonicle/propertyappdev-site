01 Enterprise AI Adoption

AI Adoption Programme

Designed and led a structured AI adoption programme across a RICS-regulated chartered surveying firm.

As Senior Property Manager at a RICS-regulated chartered surveying firm, I identified a significant opportunity to integrate AI into the firm's professional workflows. With no external brief, no allocated budget, and no prior precedent within the business, I designed and led the full rollout of Anthropic Claude Team across a five-seat pilot. This included managing seat allocation, billing, and access controls, and producing a formal AI adoption strategy report presented to senior partners.

The practical output was a set of structured training sessions delivered to colleagues, alongside prompt frameworks and workflow guides covering property management use cases: LPE1 conveyancing enquiries, Section 20 consultation documentation, correspondence drafting, and lease interpretation. These are not demonstrations; they are in active daily use by five members of staff.

In parallel, I built an AI-assisted drone survey reporting workflow. Field inspection recordings and photographs captured on-site are processed through Claude to generate structured reports, replacing a largely manual process and reducing report turnaround time substantially. This workflow combines the drone survey operations described below with applied Claude API use.

Context RICS-regulated chartered surveying firm, approximately 23 residential leasehold developments under management
Scope Adoption strategy, training, prompt frameworks, workflow integration, drone survey reporting pipeline
Status Live — in active use across the firm
02 Full-Stack Development

AI Booking Platform

Designed and built a production AI-powered booking and customer engagement platform for a small business client.

I designed and built a complete booking platform for a small business client, deployed on Netlify and in live operational use. The platform handles the full customer journey from initial enquiry through to confirmed appointment, using an AI chat assistant as the primary interface for conversational booking.

The stack is HTML with Netlify serverless functions in Node.js, Netlify Blobs for persistent booking storage, Resend for transactional email with Google Calendar link generation, and the Anthropic Claude API (claude-sonnet model) with vision capability. The AI assistant interprets customer enquiries in natural language, manages availability, prevents double-bookings through server-side logic, and hands off confirmed bookings to an automated email confirmation flow.

An admin dashboard sits behind authentication, allowing the client to view, manage, and export booking records. The platform replaced a manual phone and email enquiry process and has handled live bookings since deployment.

Stack HTML, Node.js, Netlify Functions, Netlify Blobs, Resend API, Anthropic Claude API
Features Conversational AI booking, double-booking prevention, automated email confirmations, admin dashboard
Status Live — in active use by client
03 Android & AI Development

Property Inspection App

Built a bespoke Android application to replace paper-based property inspections at a RICS-regulated surveying firm.

Property inspections at the firm previously relied on handwritten notes taken during site visits, transcribed into reports manually after the fact. I designed and built an Android application to replace the entire workflow, from on-site recording through to a delivered report.

The inspector records voice narrations during the inspection and photographs areas of concern. The app transcribes the audio via Deepgram, then passes the transcriptions to Claude Sonnet, which classifies each observation into the appropriate report section. Photographs are analysed by Claude Opus, which assigns each image to the relevant section and generates professional observation text for any area that was photographed but not narrated. A branded Word document is compiled from the output and emailed directly to the relevant parties on completion.

The stack is a Capacitor Android application, offline-first using SQLite with sync to Supabase on completion. The backend is a Node.js Express server deployed on Railway, handling transcription, AI classification and analysis, report generation via LibreOffice, and email delivery via Resend. The first live field test was completed in May 2026 on a real property inspection with a colleague.

Stack Capacitor Android, Node.js/Express, Supabase, Deepgram, Claude API (Opus + Sonnet), LibreOffice, Resend, Railway
Application Internal tool for property inspections at a RICS-regulated chartered surveying firm
Status Field tested — live inspection completed May 2026
04 Drone Operations & AI Integration

Drone Survey Operations

Commercial building inspection using UAV under CAA permitted operations, with AI-assisted report generation.

I conduct facade and roof inspections of residential leasehold developments using a DJI Mini 5 Pro UAV. At sub-250g, the aircraft operates under CAA permitted operations rules without requirement for a GVC or PfCO. This makes it practical for routine property inspection work without the overhead of a full commercial operator certification framework.

The operational workflow produces high-resolution imagery and field recordings that are used directly in maintenance planning and contractor briefings. Where in the past this kind of inspection would require scaffolding access or a manual survey from ground level, the drone survey provides comprehensive facade coverage at a fraction of the cost and time.

The reporting side of this workflow integrates directly with the AI adoption programme described above. Field inspection recordings and photographs are uploaded to Claude, which generates structured inspection reports to a defined template. This has substantially reduced the time between completing a survey and having a usable written report, and has improved consistency across report outputs.

Aircraft DJI Mini 5 Pro (sub-250g, CAA permitted operations)
Application Facade and roof inspection for residential leasehold developments
AI integration Field recordings and photographs processed through Claude to generate structured survey reports
